Software Development & Technology Transfer

Groundwater Modeling System (GMS)

Directed the development of GMS, a state-of-the-art three-dimensional modeling environment for groundwater applications. GMS integrates site characterization tools, geostatistics, solid modeling, and interfaces to multiple groundwater models including MODFLOW, MODPATH, MT3DMS, SEAM3D, RT3D, FEMWATER, SEEP2D, and ADH. The software has been adopted by over 8,000 organizations in more than 100 countries and has been described as "the most comprehensive and sophisticated groundwater modeling software available."

Arc Hydro Groundwater Data Model

Co-developed Arc Hydro Groundwater, a comprehensive GIS-based data model and toolset for groundwater applications. Published comprehensive reference manual through ESRI Press (2011). The system provides standardized approaches for organizing, analyzing, and visualizing groundwater data within GIS environments.
